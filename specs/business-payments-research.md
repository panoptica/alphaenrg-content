## 1. No-Business Crypto Options (Fastest)

### Coinbase Commerce
- **Setup time:** Minutes
- **Fees:** 1% per transaction
- **Accepts:** BTC, ETH, USDC, DAI, and more
- **Payout:** Direct to your Coinbase account or external wallet
- **Pros:** Instant setup, no KYB required, trusted brand
- **Cons:** Centralized, requires Coinbase account
- **Link:** https://commerce.coinbase.com

### BTCPay Server (Self-Hosted)
- **Setup time:** 1-2 hours (if you host it)
- **Fees:** 0% (you run it)
- **Accepts:** BTC, Lightning, some altcoins
- **Payout:** Direct to your wallet (you hold keys)
- **Pros:** Fully self-sovereign, no KYC, open source
- **Cons:** Requires server, more technical
- **Link:** https://btcpayserver.org
- **Hosting:** Can run on Mac Mini or a $5/mo VPS

### Request Network
- **Setup time:** Minutes
- **Fees:** Low (on-chain fees only)
- **Accepts:** ETH, stablecoins, multiple chains
- **Payout:** Direct to wallet
- **Pros:** Invoice system, crypto-native
- **Link:** https://request.network

### Solana Pay
- **Setup time:** Minutes (just a wallet)
- **Fees:** ~$0.00025 per transaction
- **Accepts:** USDC (Solana), SOL
- **Payout:** Instant to your Solana wallet
- **Pros:** Fastest, cheapest, QR code payments
- **Cons:** Solana ecosystem only
- **Link:** https://solanapay.com

### Recommendation for Quick Start
**Use Coinbase Commerce** for immediate setup, or **Solana Pay** if you want the lowest fees and have a Solana wallet.

## 2. UK Limited Company (24-48 hours)

### Direct via Companies House
- **Cost:** £12 (online filing)
- **Time:** 24-48 hours
- **What you need:**
  - Company name (check availability on Companies House)
  - Registered address (can be your home, or use a service ~£50/yr)
  - Director details (you)
  - SIC code: 63110 (Data processing) or 62090 (IT consultancy)
- **Link:** https://www.gov.uk/limited-company-formation

### Formation Agents (Faster, More Support)
- **1st Formations:** £12.99, includes digital docs
- **Rapid Formations:** £14.99, often same-day
- **What they handle:** Filing, memorandum, articles, certificate

### Post-Formation Requirements
- Register for Corporation Tax (within 3 months)
- Open business bank account (Tide, Starling, Monzo Business)
- Consider VAT registration if revenue > £85k

### Registered Office Options
- Your home address (visible on public register)
- Virtual office: ~£50-100/year (London address)
- Accountant's address: Often included with accounting services

## 3. UK Ltd + Stripe (2-3 days total)

### Once Ltd is formed:
1. **Open business bank account** — Tide or Starling (same day)
2. **Apply for Stripe** — https://stripe.com/gb
   - Needs: Company number, bank account, ID verification
   - Approval: Usually 1-2 days
3. **Start accepting cards**

### Stripe Fees (UK)
- 1.4% + 20p (UK cards)
- 2.9% + 20p (international cards)
- Invoicing available
- Subscription billing available

## 4. US LLC via Stripe Atlas (1-2 weeks)

### If you want US presence:
- **Cost:** $500 one-time
- **Includes:** Delaware LLC, EIN, Stripe account, bank account (SVB or Mercury)
- **Time:** 1-2 weeks
- **Pros:** US banking, easier for US customers, credibility
- **Cons:** Expensive, US tax filing requirements
- **Link:** https://stripe.com/atlas
